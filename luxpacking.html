About Us

Packaging offer business the ability to present products or a concept.Well engineered rigid boxes provide a unique branding opportunity. Luxpacking specializes in manufacturing customized rigid boxes for a variety of verticals including Gifts, Confectioneries, Fashion, Beauty, Cosmetics, Electronic, Liquor and Weddings. We design in-house and offer a single stop solution to develop customized rigid boxes. Luxpacking is a brand of GreenArt, a company found in 2011. We are women owned with majority of workforce being women.

Case Study - Park Hyatt Hyderabad

Park Hyatt Hyderabad, even after three years of inception humbly boasts about its services which fancy the eyes of every customer. It is the first international Hyatt brand in Hyderabad operating since April 2012. Due to the wide array of customized services, it has managed to redeem its loyal customers who swear by the brand âHYATTâ. Services here are rendered according to the clients taste as customer satisfaction is an important criterion for the hotel.
But problem in the heaven soon aroused in the Food and Beverages section. They had grave problem in sourcing packaging for their Confectionary products and Beverages. The demand for the packaging of products such as chocolates, macaroons, cookies, cakes, wine bottles etc, all high-ended, escalated very fast. The packaging was expected to complement and meet the brand standards. And finding the right company to outsource turned into an elephant task. Reason? There are hardly any premium box manufacturers in India and it was very hard for the Hotel management to find an appropriate partner.
After an extensive search they struck gold with GREEN ART. The company started with the idea of replacing the not-so-cool and non-biodegradable plastic bags and boxes with something that is appealing and also environmental friendly. The GREEN ART team saw an opportunity to solve the problem of Hyatt by providing luxury packaging solution for all the required products. They worked closely with the Director of Food and Beverages, Chef, Brand Communications Director and the Purchase team to perfectly mould a vision into reality.
Working hand in hand helped in addressing and solving the problem. The GREEN ART team was thoroughly lauded for its remarkable work and rewarded with testimonials from the potential clients. Also, the company was referred to other premium brands. The relationship started even before the opening of the Hyatt, i.e. 2011, and has been only strengthening till date.
